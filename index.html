Course Overview

This course covers the theory and practice of distributed optimization for modern machine learning and AI systems. Topics include consensus-based algorithms, federated learning, parallel and asynchronous methods, and optimization for large-scale foundation models.

Prerequisites: Linear algebra, probability, and a basic understanding of machine learning.
